Return Dispersion by Sector

The purpose of this visualization is to compare sector-level volatility. By isolating the median return and the interquartile range, this graph allows you to determine which industries offer consistent "IPO pops" versus those that are highly unpredictable. It is designed to reveal whether a specific company's performance was an industry-wide trend or an anomaly.
